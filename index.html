Agnès Pérez-Millan

Barcelona, Spain · agper-remove-ez@recerca.clinic.cat

Hello, I’m Agnès!

I’m graduated in Physics with a MSc in Bioinformatics and Biostatistics. Currently, I’m working as a Pre-doctoral researcher between Alzheimer’s disease and other cognitive disorders Unit at the Hospital Clínic of Barcelona and the Bioengineering Unit at the University of Barcelona. Also, I’m a teaching assistant of Physics courses at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia.

The topic of my thesis is the study of neurodegenerative diseases through the processing of neuroimaging cross-sectional and longitudinal studies combining it with machine learning, bioinformatics and biostatistics analysis.
